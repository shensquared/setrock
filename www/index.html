SIPB Petrock

Petrock is a new service that allows your sites to log users in with their MIT account via Touchstone.  Petrock is intended as an alternative to IS&T's now defunct OpenID Connect Pilot and intended to be more accessible than CSAIL's Shimmer or setting up a Shibboleth server.



The Petrock service currently runs on a ROC-RK3328-CC microcomputer in the SIPB server room.

See the About page for documentation on enabling your site or service.

For more details, contact us by email (petrock@) or in person at SIPB's weekly meetings (M19:30 in W20-557).
